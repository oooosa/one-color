We focus on making high quality and fun games that are loved by everyone. A game is not a kind of software, it should be fun and engaging that's why we make sure that it should have these ingredients. We make Mobile & HTML5 games that works fluidly on cross-platform devices
GAMES
PASS ME
Pass me is a casual game, where your goal is to pass the ball to other person, while the game progresses the persons will stand on difficult places to make the gameplay even more challenging. Collect stars to unlock amazing outfits and ball skins.
BROFIST.IO
Brofist.io is a multiplayer realtime game where you have to co-operate with your brothers (other players) to complete challenges, Your main goal is to reach the end gate with co-operation. It has 2 extra modes 'Hide n Seek' & 'Sandbox', currently it's our flagship game
CRAZY COLORS
It's an addictive arcade game, Tap Tap on screen to move the ball up, after crossing each obstacle your ball color switches, Make sure you have to cross each obstacle with the same color as your ball have.
DISCOLORS
Discolors is a clever brain teaser game where the player has to fill polygons with corresponding colors by dragging them from an already filled polygon. Sometimes the needed colors are not on the board and the player has to mix colors
SHIPZ.IO
shipz.io is turn based multiplayer battleship game to fight with real people. Arrange your ships and get into battle. Your main goal is to sink all 10 ships of enemy before he sink yours. First one to sink all ships will win the battle.
SILLY SNAKE
It's casual simple game where you hold the screen to lift the snake up and release to let it down, by doing this you have to cross infinite coming obstacles
YELLOW ADVENTURE
Help the tiny yellow ball to reach the exit point, It has 30 different challenges with varying difficulty and variety
